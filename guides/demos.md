# Demos

## What are the demos?

The demos are folders with scenes, scripts, and/or other files that are included with the FishNet package. They are **NOT** complete game examples or projects but simply examples of how to use some of FishNet's features. You can find them under the **(FishNet/Demos)** folder within your project.
